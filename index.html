Game Play

Breathing fire, flying around, defeating enemies. This is what you can do in our amazing game called “Snow Dragons”. Your dragon is trapped in an unfortunate snow storm. The only way to survive is to avoid or shoot at the snow. The more snow you shoot, the higher your score will be. But be carefull. Your Dragon only has 3 lives. If a snowball hits the dragon a life goes down.

How to play

push to navigate around

press to shoot
